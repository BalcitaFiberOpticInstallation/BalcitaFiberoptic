About Us

BALCITA Fiber Optic Installation Services is a project-based fiber optic and network infrastructure contractor providing installation, testing, structured cabling, rack buildout, and technical support services for enterprise environments. The company supports projects across hospitals, data centers, commercial buildings, and corporate sites — with experience in fiber backbone termination, fusion splicing, OTDR and Fluke testing, endpoint deployment, network validation, and project documentation.

Company Type

Project-based fiber optic and network infrastructure contractor and subcontractor, supporting client projects including work done for TechConnect I.T. Solutions.

Industries Served

Hospitals, data centers, commercial buildings, and corporate environments across Metro Manila and surrounding areas.

Documentation

We provide complete project documentation including bidding estimates, bill of quantities, as-built plans, test results, and progress reports.

Technical Expertise

Certified technicians with hands-on experience in OTDR, Fluke, LinkWare, and Anritsu testing, fusion splicing, and enterprise network validation.
