About Us

BALCITA Fiber Optic Installation Services is a project-based fiber optic and network infrastructure contractor specializing in installation, splicing, testing, structured cabling, rack buildout, and technical support for enterprise-grade environments.

We support projects across hospitals, data centers, commercial properties, telecom facilities, and corporate sites throughout the Philippines. Our hands-on experience includes fiber backbone termination, fusion splicing, OTDR and Fluke testing, endpoint deployment, network validation, and project documentation.
